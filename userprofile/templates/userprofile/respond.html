What is pytagora?

Pytagora is a service that helps job seekers to get a reference from a previous employer.

Pytagora also helps recruiters and employers to get a more objective response pattern from references in a recruiting process and saves them time.

What's in it for me?

Pytagora lets its users store their references forever.
